How to Play

Welcome to Teritree, this help page goes over how to play the game.

W - Move Forward
A - Move Left
S - Move Down
D - Move Right

LEFT CLICK - Pick / Place Tree

Press T to create a town, this will take you to your own area, then move out a bit until you see the flashing claim area. Place more trees into the area to get points. Whoever has the most points at the end of the week wins.

About the Game

Trees will naturally generate around the world, you are able to create a town and move trees into your claimed area. Each week, the map resets and the town with the most trees in it will come first in the leaderboard.

You’re able to expand your town to get as many trees as possible and invite more players in to help with your mission, be careful though as players are also able to take trees from your town!

Different trees give your town different amounts of points, keep an eye out for rare trees.

How to Join

To play, you’ll need to create an account, then you will be redirected to the main game where you’ll be prompted to create a town or join an existing open one (if one exists that meets this criteria). You’ll then be able to follow a tutorial and be shown how to play the game.

At the end of the week, if you’ve won the tournament you’ll receive a notification the next time you log in.

Updates

To view recent updates that have been made to the game, check the changelog. The current version of the game is 0.10, adding More biomes, party features and bug fixes.

The game will automatically download the latest update as you rejoin the game, meaning you don’t have to do anything to stay on the latest version.
